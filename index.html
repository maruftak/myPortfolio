01. about

Who I Am

I'm a full-stack developer and cybersecurity researcher who builds things that matter. My journey spans both offensive security research and production-grade web engineering — a rare combination that gives me a unique perspective on building secure systems.

My final year project was an Advanced IoT Camera Honeypot — a fully instrumented decoy system deployed to attract, capture, and analyze real-world attack traffic. The companion tool, IoTLogAnalyzer, transforms raw packet captures into structured threat intelligence: classifying botnet families, mapping CVE exploits, and generating geographic attacker profiles.

On the web side, I've shipped MenuBangla, a restaurant management and menu platform, and TurkStay, a licensed Turkish tour operator app with custom trip building, Stripe payments, and multi-day itineraries — both live, both used by real people.
