02. about

Who I Am

I'm an ethical hacker and penetration tester at heart. I love breaking into systems — legally — understanding how attackers think, and using that knowledge to build more secure software. Cybersecurity isn't just a skill for me, it's a passion.

My final year project was an Advanced IoT Camera Honeypot — a fully instrumented decoy system deployed to attract and capture real-world attack traffic. The companion tool, IoTLogAnalyzer, turns raw packet captures into structured threat intelligence: classifying botnet families, mapping CVE exploits, and generating geographic attacker profiles.

I also build production web apps — MenuBangla and TurkStay are live and used by real people. But my true calling is offensive security: finding vulnerabilities before the bad guys do.
